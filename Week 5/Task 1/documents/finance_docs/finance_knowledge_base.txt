# Finance Department Knowledge Base

## Expense Reimbursement

### How to File a Reimbursement

To file an expense reimbursement:

1. **Gather Documentation**
   - Original receipts (digital or physical)
   - Business purpose description
   - Project/cost center code
   - Manager approval (for amounts over $500)

2. **Submit Through Portal**
   - Log in to finance.company.com
   - Click "Expense Reimbursement"
   - Upload receipts
   - Fill out expense details
   - Submit for approval

3. **Approval Workflow**
   - Manager reviews (1-2 business days)
   - Finance reviews (2-3 business days)
   - Payment processed (5-7 business days)
   - Direct deposit to your registered account

4. **Reimbursable Expenses**
   - Business travel (flights, hotels, meals)
   - Client entertainment (with justification)
   - Office supplies
   - Professional development courses
   - Mileage (current rate: $0.65/mile)

5. **Non-Reimbursable Expenses**
   - Personal items
   - Alcoholic beverages (unless client meeting)
   - Traffic violations
   - Commuting costs

**Submission Deadline:** Within 60 days of expense date

## Purchase Orders

### How to Create a Purchase Order

For purchases over $1,000:

1. **Initiate Request**
   - Go to Procurement Portal (procurement.company.com)
   - Click "Create Purchase Order"
   - Enter vendor information
   - Add item details and quantities

2. **Required Approvals**
   - Up to $5,000: Manager approval only
   - $5,000-$25,000: Manager + Finance Director
   - Over $25,000: Manager + Finance Director + CFO

3. **Processing Time**
   - Standard: 3-5 business days
   - Urgent: 1-2 business days (requires justification)

4. **Vendor Payment Terms**
   - Net 30 standard
   - Net 60 for large orders
   - Early payment discounts evaluated case-by-case

## Travel Expense Policy

### Business Travel Expenses

**Flight Booking:**
- Use company travel portal (travel.company.com)
- Economy class for flights under 5 hours
- Business class approved for international flights over 5 hours
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible

**Hotel Allowances:**
- Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, LA): Up to $250/night
- Tier 2 cities: Up to $150/night
- Tier 3 cities: Up to $100/night

**Meal Per Diem:**
- Breakfast: $15
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $40
- Total daily maximum: $80

**Ground Transportation:**
- Taxis/rideshare: Reasonable amounts
- Rental cars: Approved for multi-day trips
- Parking: Keep all receipts
