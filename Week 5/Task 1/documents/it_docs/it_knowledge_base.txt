# IT Department Knowledge Base

## VPN Setup Guide

### How to Set Up VPN

To set up the company VPN on your device:

1. **Download VPN Client**
   - Windows: Download Cisco AnyConnect from IT Portal
   - Mac: Download Cisco AnyConnect from IT Portal
   - Mobile: Download Cisco AnyConnect from App Store/Play Store

2. **Installation**
   - Run the installer with administrator privileges
   - Follow the on-screen instructions
   - Restart your device if prompted

3. **Configuration**
   - Open Cisco AnyConnect
   - Enter VPN address: vpn.company.com
   - Use your company email as username
   - Use your regular password with 2FA

4. **Troubleshooting**
   - If connection fails, check your internet connection
   - Ensure firewall allows VPN traffic
   - Contact IT Helpdesk at ext. 1234 for assistance

## Laptop Request Process

### How to Request a New Laptop

To request a new laptop or replacement:

1. **Submit Request**
   - Go to IT Portal (it.company.com)
   - Click "Hardware Requests"
   - Select "New Laptop Request"
   - Fill out the request form

2. **Required Information**
   - Business justification
   - Manager approval (will be auto-requested)
   - Preferred specifications
   - Urgency level

3. **Approval Process**
   - Manager reviews and approves
   - IT reviews technical requirements
   - Procurement processes order
   - Typical timeline: 2-3 weeks

4. **Pickup and Setup**
   - You'll receive email when ready
   - Pick up from IT desk (Building A, Floor 1)
   - IT will help with initial setup
   - Old laptop must be returned if replacement

**Standard Laptop Options:**
- Dell Latitude 5420 (Standard)
- MacBook Pro 14" (Development/Design)
- Lenovo ThinkPad X1 (Executive)

## Password Reset

### How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Go to password.company.com
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your company email
4. Verify via SMS or email code
5. Create new password (must meet complexity requirements)

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
